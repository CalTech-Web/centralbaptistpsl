## PAGE: OUR STORY (/our-story)

**Heading:** Our Story

**Subheading:** HISTORY - CENTRAL BAPTIST CHURCH

**Mission Statement:**
"Striving to be a community of faith who are banded together by our love, mission, and purpose to proclaim the gospel of Jesus Christ."

**History Content:**
The church began with 6 couples and three friends meeting in a private home in Windmill Point in February of 1978. It relocated to a Recreation Hall in August 1979 and received sponsorship from First Baptist Church of Ft. Pierce.

A groundbreaking ceremony occurred on February 22, 1981, with construction completed by August 1981. First services in the new building were held October 4, 1981, with dedication on November 1, 1981.

The Mission was constituted as Central Baptist Church on September 30, 1984. Various ministry programs expanded through the 1980s, including Women's Missionary Union (1983), Baptist Men's Ministry, children's programs, and bus ministry (1985-1986).

A fellowship hall and Kids Worship center addition was completed in 2023.

**Leadership Note:**
Pastor Dr. Nick Manzie ("Brother Nick") - serving since 2003, initially as Chaplain with Christian Motorsports International.

## PAGE: OUR PASTOR (/our-pastor)

**Heading:** Our Pastor

**Image:** `/images/pastor-1030x771-1-1024x767.jpg`

### Dr. Nick Manzie

**Bio:**
Dr. Nick Manzie (known as "Brother Nick") has served in ministry since 2003, beginning as a Chaplain with Christian Motorsports International. He became Lead Pastor of Central Baptist Church in August 2019.

**Background:**
Born in Newark, NJ to parents of Puerto Rican and Italian heritage. Moved to Miami in 1997, where he met his wife Jessica in that same year. The family relocated to Port St. Lucie in 2004, then Kentucky in 2015, returning to Port St. Lucie in 2018.

**Family & Personal Interests:**
Married to Jessica with five adult children: Sabrina, Bridgette, Selah, Adam, and Nick (Trey). Four grandchildren. Enjoys beach trips, ethnic restaurants (particularly Japanese food), hiking, and family activities.

**Additional Roles:**
Serves as Chaplain at Port St Lucie Police Department. Officiates football, basketball, and baseball for local high schools and colleges. Former professional X-Games style roller-blader who retired upon his first daughter's birth.

## PAGE: WHAT WE BELIEVE (/what-we-believe)

**Heading:** What We Believe

**Image:** `/images/what-we-believe.jpg`

### Core Beliefs:

1. **About God** - "God is the Creator and Ruler of the universe. He has eternally existed in three persons: the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit."

2. **About Mankind** - Humans are made in God's image, but "marred by an attitude of disobedience toward God called sin."

3. **About Eternity** - People exist forever, either "separated from God by sin" (Hell) or "in union with God" (Heaven).

4. **About Jesus Christ** - "Jesus is the Son of God...offered Himself as the perfect sacrifice for the sins of all by dying on the cross."

5. **About Salvation** - "Salvation is a gift from God to mankind...only by trusting in Jesus Christ...can we be saved."

6. **About Eternal Security** - Believers maintain salvation "by the grace and power of God, not by the self-effort of the Christian."

7. **About the Holy Spirit** - The Spirit "lives in every Christian from the moment of salvation" and provides "power for living."

8. **About the Bible** - Scripture is "God's word to all mankind...truth without any mixture of error."
